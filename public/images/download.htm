    Sleekjob

    Sleekjob is an on-demand software service which allows businesses to grow by connecting them to trained African freelance software developers. All that you have to do is to tell us what you need, and then we send you a quote, prepare milestones and get a team of developers to bring it to reality. We will do all the project management on your behalf and offer you technical advice.

    Sleekjob trains its developers in its Academy in Accra, Ghana where they learn web and mobile development and most importantly the soft skills needed to work in teams. Successful graduates of the school become freelance developers on Sleekjob.
